ABOUT

I'm Jacob Yelton, a full-stack software engineer and mountain enthusiast. The thirst for freedom defines my person and informs my perspectives on design.

I arrived in the software world by way of geographic information science and data analytics. My previous background includes GIS/geospatial research, ArcPy scripting, relational database management + administration, software support/UAT, and scientific field data collection.

I am now firmly planted in the world of software + web application development. I will graduate from the University of Denver Full-Stack Web Development in November 2018.

Whenever possible, I can be found climbing mountains, rock, snow, and trails. Sweating for the next objective, always and forever.
